How to Play

Goal: Fill every cell with I or O.

  • No more than 2 consecutive same values in any row or column.
  • Each row and column must have equal numbers of I and O.
  • = between cells means both cells have the same value.
  • × between cells means both cells have opposite values.

Controls: Click a cell to cycle: empty → I → O → empty. Errors show in red in real-time.

How to Play

Goal: Place exactly 1 star ⭐ in each row, each column, and each colored region.

  • Stars cannot be adjacent to each other — not even diagonally.
  • Click once = ❌ mark (star can't go here).
  • Click again = ⭐ place a star.
  • Click again = clear the cell.
